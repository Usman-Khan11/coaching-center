About Us



Excellent Coaching Centre is an institution established in the year 2001 with the aim of providing quality education to students so that they can make their own way in this competitive world. Till today Excellent Coaching centre along with its sister concern 'Career Alley' provide guidance to thousands of students in Pakistan. Many of our students are now successfully placed in their respective field. The sole objective and vision of our teachers are to provide quality education with concept building techniques so that the students can excel in their careers.
