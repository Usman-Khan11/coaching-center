Chairman





Prof Hamza Ahmed Faridi

Chairman of Excellent Coaching Centre

MSc Chemistry (University of Karachi)

It gives me great pleasure and a sense of pride as I look back in the past when I first laid the foundation of Excellent Coaching Centre in a four roomed premises which later on evolved into a prestigious institution and now boasts of two purpose built campuses. In 2001 we diversified our educational portfolio and established Excellent Schooling System. My team comprises of some highly competent and experienced individuals including Basim Ahmed (Director Excellent caoching Centre & Excellent Schooling System and Ms Miriam Arsalan (Director PSS Montessori). They both have taken forward my dream of establishing a private pre-primary – O’levels school and turned it into a state of the art educational institute imparting quality education.<

At Excellent Schooling System, we offer admissions in pre-primary, primary & secondary (O-levels/matric) classes. We are fortunate to have one of the best teachers in town headed by a very competent principal and a team of coordinators. Besides studies, we have Extra and Co-curricular activities taking place within and outside the campus quiet frequently round the year. Our students have excelled in both the Matric and Cambridge board examinations.

I thank and pray to God for His support and blessings and I hold in the highest esteem, all those individuals who supported me and became my strength throughout.
